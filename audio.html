The audio is important.




Audio can be more important than you think. You can divide audio in multiple segments. You have: Music, Monolougue, sound effects and the game sounds.


What i did for music was that i watched a video made by Jaiden Animations on YouTube. And i found out that almost every mood or scene change the music changes with it. Becouse what i did was just repeating the same music by Noam Levy over and over until the video was over.
So what i did was: I downloaded a lot Copyright free music and divided them into different emotions. Like calm, Determined, Exiting, Energetic, etc. And that way i can put music under every segment and it will accentuate the video instead of being just background music.


Monologue is also an important part of your video. I realized that my voice was quite monotone while talking into my microphone. So to change that i realized: If i talk to my friends i talk way different than if i talk to OBS. So what i have to do is just pretend that i am actually talking to someone that cares about what you have to talk about! Becouse otherwise people don't think you are interested in them.


Sound effects are not that important to create a good video. But if you do it right you can use it to accentuate different parts of your video, like a change or transition. I personally don't use that many sound effects, becouse i don't think they do that much in my videos.


If i am recording a video you can change the settings in OBS so your voice lines and game audio will be exported so if you put it inside of the edditing software you have 2 different audio streams. This can be very usefull if you want to add effects to your voice without affecting the game sound.
